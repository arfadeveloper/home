ONE HOMES

Who We Are

One Homes is a holistic placemaker focused on developing high-quality, lifestyle-centric living spaces in Pakistan. Established by a group of international real estate experts, the company leads the market with the highest standards of international best practices and a customer-centric approach. 

 

The Live beyond Ideology

Our ideology of “Live Beyond” goes beyond the traditional notion of opulence and encompasses a deeper understanding of ‘destination making’. We build spaces that inspire and empower people to live better through fostering human connection, happiness, health and well-being.

We create living experiences that surpass material possessions and encompass the intangible aspects that enrich our lives and touch our souls. We build not just homes but destinations providing a holistic lifestyle, where you and your family can live your best life and create lasting memories.

Let’s not just live, let’s thrive.

Destination Making

Destination making is a term that we at One Homes have coined to describe our unique process of real estate design and development. Our aim is to create not just homes, but complete destinations that provide an all-encompassing lifestyle, where you and your family can live your best life and create lasting memories.

Embody Integrity

Our success is derived from our commitment to be the best versions of ourselves. We persevere through challenges that come our way and sacrifice short term rewards for long term success. We understand the treasures of greatness are found not on the other side of a rainbow but at the end of a long road of hard work, dedication and sacrifice.
